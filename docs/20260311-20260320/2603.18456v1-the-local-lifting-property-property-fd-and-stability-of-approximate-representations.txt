# Title:The Local Lifting Property, Property FD, and stability of approximate representations

Authors:[Francesco Fournier-Facio](https://arxiv.org/search/math?searchtype=author&query=Fournier-Facio,+F), [Rufus Willett](https://arxiv.org/search/math?searchtype=author&query=Willett,+R)

View a PDF of the paper titled The Local Lifting Property, Property FD, and stability of approximate representations, by Francesco Fournier-Facio and Rufus Willett

[View PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.18456v1)[HTML (experimental)](https://arxiv.org/html/2603.18456v1)
> Abstract:We establish Kirchberg's Local Lifting Property and Lubotzky-Shalom's Property FD for classes of finitely generated groups of central importance in geometric and combinatorial group theory: 3-manifold groups, limit groups, and certain one-relator groups and right-angled Artin groups. We deduce that such groups are very flexibly stable, with respect to normalized unitarily invariant norms. The exposition is made accessible to operator algebraists and group theorists alike.
