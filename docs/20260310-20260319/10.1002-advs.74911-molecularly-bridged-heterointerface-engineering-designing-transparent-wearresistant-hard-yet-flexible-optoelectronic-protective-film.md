## 摘要
透明、硬而柔韧的薄膜是支撑和保护柔性光电装置的关键组件。然而，目前的材料面临着重大的技术挑战：聚合物薄膜硬度低且易屈服，而无机玻璃脆且不具柔韧性。在此，通过共价键界面工程策略开发了一种具有类玻璃硬度、类聚合物柔韧性和类陶瓷耐磨性的双层结构透明薄膜，该薄膜采用定义明确的多面体低聚倍半硅氧烷（POSS）涂层作为硬层，并以无色聚酰亚胺基底作为柔性层。通过对其具有大无机笼结构的母体 T10-MMA 进行光交联或热交联制备的 PT10MMA 涂层提供了强大的机械性能。热敏无色聚酰亚胺（CPI）薄膜支持 T10-MMA 在其上的自交联以及两者之间的界面交联，同时还缓解了 PT10MMA 的应力诱导断裂并增强了其应变耐受性。PT10MMA 与 CPI 层之间强大的异质界面结合强度确保了保留两层的固有优势，同时也促进了协同功能的出现。因此，该薄膜实现了 >89.6% 的透光率、7H 硬度、>500 次钢丝绒磨损循环、高达 50°C 的无蠕变性能，以及在 200,000 次弯曲循环（1 mm 弯曲半径）后无折痕。

## Abstract
ABSTRACT Transparent, hard yet flexible films serve as critical components for supporting and protecting flexible optoelectronic devices. However, current materials face significant technical challenges where polymer films exhibit low hardness and yield easily, and inorganic glass is brittle and inflexible. Herein, a bilayer‐structured transparent film with glass‐like hardness, polymer‐like flexibility, and ceramic‐like wear resistance is developed via a covalent bonding interface engineering strategy, employing a well‐defined polyhedral oligomeric silsesquioxane (POSS) coating as a hard layer, and a colorless polyimide substrate as a flexible layer. The PT10MMA coating, prepared by photo‐ or thermal‐crosslinking of its precursor T10‐MMA featuring a large inorganic cage, offers robust mechanical properties. The thermosensitive colorless polyimide (CPI) film supports the self‐crosslinking of T10‐MMA on it and interfacial crosslinking between the two, while also alleviating the stress‐induced fracture of PT10MMA and enhancing its strain tolerance. The robust heterogeneous interfacial bonding strength between PT10MMA and CPI layers ensures the retention of the inherent advantages of both layers, while also promoting the emergence of synergistic functionalities. Consequently, the film achieves >89.6% transmittance, 7H hardness, >500 steel wool abrasion cycles, creep‐free up to 50°C, and no creases after 200 000 bending cycles (1 mm bending radius).
