## 摘要
我们提出了一种用于发现功能材料的机器学习加速高通量 (HTP) 工作流。作为测试案例，我们对四元及全 d 区 Heusler 化合物进行了筛选，以寻找具有大磁晶各向异性能 (E_aniso) 的稳定化合物。利用 eSEN-30M-OAM 原子间势进行结构优化以及形成能和凸包能量的评估，而局部磁矩、声子稳定性、磁稳定性和 E_aniso 则由在我们的 DxMag Heusler 数据库上训练的 eSEN 模型进行预测。为了提高准确性，采用了冻结迁移学习策略。通过机器学习高通量 (ML-HTP) 工作流识别出的候选化合物经过密度泛函理论验证，证实了高预测精度。我们还基准测试了不同通用机器学习原子间势 (uMLIPs) 的性能，讨论了局部磁矩预测的保真度，并演示了通过通用原子间势的迁移学习对未见元素的泛化能力。

## Abstract
Abstract We present a machine learning-accelerated high-throughput (HTP) workflow for the discovery of functional materials. As a test case, quaternary and all- d Heusler compounds were screened for stable compounds with large magnetocrystalline anisotropy energy ( E aniso ). Structure optimization and evaluation of formation energy and energy above the convex hull were performed using the eSEN-30M-OAM interatomic potential, while local magnetic moments, phonon stability, magnetic stability, and E aniso were predicted by eSEN models trained on our DxMag Heusler database. A frozen transfer learning strategy was employed to improve accuracy. Candidate compounds identified by the ML-HTP workflow were validated with density functional theory, confirming high predictive precision. We also benchmark the performance of different uMLIPs, discuss the fidelity of local magnetic moment prediction, and demonstrate generalization to unseen elements via transfer learning from a universal interatomic potential.
